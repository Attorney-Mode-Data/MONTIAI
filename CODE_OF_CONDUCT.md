# Proprietary Code of Conduct — MONTI PROJECTS
## In the best interest of JOHN CHARLES MONTI & EXCLUSIVELY

### Our Pledge

In the interest of fostering a focused and productive environment, we as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in our project and community a constructive experience for everyone, always aligned with the **exclusive best interest of JOHN CHARLES MONTI**. All activities, contributions, and interactions shall respect the vision and leadership of Monti.

### Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Using respectful and clear communication.
- Being open to constructive feedback.
- Focusing on what is best for the project and its owner.
- Showing consideration for other community members.

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- The use of abusive, discriminatory, or threatening language.
- Personal attacks, trolling, or public harassment.
- Publishing private information without explicit consent.
- Any conduct that undermines the integrity or ownership of the project.

### Our Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards. They have the right to remove, edit, or reject contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct, or to temporarily or permanently ban any contributor whose behavior is deemed harmful to the project or its owner.

### Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces and in public spaces when an individual is representing the project. Representation includes using official project channels, social media, or acting as an appointed representative.

### Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project team at **conduct@montinode.com**. All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly, with confidentiality maintained. Enforcement actions will be determined in the sole discretion of the project owner, JOHN CHARLES MONTI, and may include warning, suspension, or permanent exclusion.
